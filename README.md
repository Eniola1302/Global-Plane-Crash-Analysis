# Global-Plane-Crash-Analysis
This analysis examines historical plane crash records to identify trends, patterns, and factors influencing aviation accidents across different regions and time periods.

## Problem Statement
The increasing number of plane crashes recorded over the years has raised concerns about aviation safety across different regions of the world. However, there is limited understanding of the factors responsible for this accidents and how there impact varies globally, within Africa and in Nigeria. This makes it difficult to determine the major causes of crashes, identify the most affected locations and understand historical accident trend. 
As a result, there is a need to:
* Identify the regions and countries with the highest number of crashes and fatalities.
* Determine the leading causes of plane crashes
* Examine how crashes occurrence has changed over time
* Compare crash patterns between the world, Africa and Nigeria.
* Identify areas requiring greater attentions to improve aviation safety.

## Analysis Objective
* Clean and prepare a large, messy real world dataset for analysis.
* Design a connected set of dashboards that move from global to national scope.
* Apply consistent branding and good data-visualization practice.
* Draw honest, evidence-based conclusions and turn them into recommendations.
